Those links you often click are not regular text. These are called hypertext and they allow you to surf the internet. Without them, there would be no surfing. We would consume websites similarly to how we read a physical book.

Hypertext is composed of two words. "hyper" meaning beyond and "text" well text. So if you had heard about this term you now know that is basically text that has beyond capabilities and it makes it possible for everyone not to read in a fixed order. You can end up in places you never thought. Isn't that cool? There is so much that we give for granted and never stop to admire.
